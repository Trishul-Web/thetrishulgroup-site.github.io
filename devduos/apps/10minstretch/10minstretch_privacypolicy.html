Devduos ("we", "us", or "our") operates the 10 Min Stretch mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

10 Min Stretch is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or physical address to use the core features of the app.

Your app preferences, completed onboarding status, and workout history are stored locally on your device using local storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains on your device.

These third parties have access to your usage data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
