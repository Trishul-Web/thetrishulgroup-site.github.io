Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

DevDuo's ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data when you use the Daily Tracker mobile application (the "Service").

1. Fully Offline & Local Storage

Daily Tracker is designed to be a 100% offline application. We do not collect, transmit, upload, or store any of your personal data or tracking metrics on our servers or any third-party servers.

All data you enter into the app—including custom trackers, folder names, logged values, and dates—is stored securely and exclusively in a local SQLite database directly on your physical device.

2. Data Retention Policy

Because your data is stored locally on your device, we do not retain or control your data. Your data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the Daily Tracker application installed.

3. How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. Because no accounts are created and no data is sent to our servers, you do not need to submit an account deletion request to us. You can permanently delete all your data at any time using one of the following methods:

  • Uninstall the App: Simply uninstalling Daily Tracker from your device will permanently delete the local database and all associated tracking data.
  • Clear App Data: Navigate to your device's Settings > Apps > Daily Tracker > Storage and tap "Clear Data". This will wipe all saved trackers and logs without uninstalling the app.

Please note: Because your data is entirely offline, once deleted, it cannot be recovered by us.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that harvest your data. The app requires no internet permissions to function.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: support@thetrishulgroup.com
