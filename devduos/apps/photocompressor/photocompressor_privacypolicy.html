Privacy Policy for Photo Compressor

Last Updated: March 2026

DevDuo's ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding the Photo Compressor application (the "Service").

1. Local Processing Guarantee

Photo Compressor is designed with privacy as a priority. All image compression, comparison, and batch processing occur locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos on any external servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your Photos: We have absolutely no access to the images you select or compress.
  • Personal Identity: We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email address for core usage.

3. Data We May Collect

To keep the app functional and bug-free, we may collect minimal, non-identifiable data:

  • Anonymous Analytics: We may gather anonymous crash reports and basic usage metrics to understand how features (like the Compare Slider or Batch Processing) are performing.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention: Because your data (such as lifetime compression statistics and app preferences) is stored exclusively on your local device and not on any external servers, we retain this data for only as long as you keep the App installed on your device. Processed images are saved directly to your device's local gallery or storage.

Data Deletion: You have full control over your data. To completely delete all of your app data, you can do either of the following:

  • Uninstall the App: Deleting the Photo Compressor app from your device will permanently erase all locally stored app data and statistics.
  • Clear App Data: You can clear the app's data through your device's system settings (e.g., Settings > Apps > Photo Compressor > Storage > Clear Data / Clear Storage).
  • Images: Any compressed images you have chosen to save to your device's gallery must be deleted manually through your device's photos app.

5. Third-Party Services

If the app contains advertisements or utilizes third-party analytics (like Google Firebase), these services may collect anonymous identifiers as dictated by their respective privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@thetrishulgroup.com
