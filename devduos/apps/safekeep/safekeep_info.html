Safekeep is a zero-knowledge, end-to-end encrypted service that allows you to store and manage your digital files securely. All encryption and decryption operations are performed exclusively on your device.

Core Security Principles

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Your Master Key is the only key that can unlock your files. It is never sent to us or stored on any server. We cannot access your data, ever.

On-Device Operations

All encryption and decryption happens exclusively on your device. Your files are only ever in their original state while you are actively viewing them.

Cross-Platform Sync

For signed-in users, your encrypted data is backed up to a private folder inside your own Google Drive account. We never have access to your Google Drive.
