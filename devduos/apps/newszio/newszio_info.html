Newszio

Global News. Custom Feeds. Zero Clutter.

Newszio is a premium news aggregator designed for professionals who want to stay ahead. With a sleek, dark-mode interface, it aggregates content from top global sources, allowing you to create custom news mixes and pin your favorite topics for instant access.

Key Features

  • Global Sources: Access news from 50+ trusted sources across India, USA, UK, and more.
  • Custom Mixes: Create your own feeds by combining keywords, countries, and categories.
  • Premium Dark Mode: A "Pure Premium Black" interface optimized for readability.
  • Smart Bookmarks: Save articles offline to your personal library.
